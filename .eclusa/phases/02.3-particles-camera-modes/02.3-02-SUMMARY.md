## Decisions Made
- Camera trait is object-safe -- `&dyn Camera` compiles and works
- Pitch/elevation clamped with epsilon 0.01 to prevent gimbal lock at poles
- FpsCamera locks Y in translate_xz (not in view_matrix) for cleaner separation
- run.rs uses transitional OrbitCamera shim when non-orbit cameras are active (Renderer::draw still takes &OrbitCamera; full polymorphic rendering deferred to future plan)
