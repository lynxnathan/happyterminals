## Decisions Made
- STL-provided normals are used when finite and non-zero length (> 1e-12); degenerate normals fall back to `flat_normal()` cross-product recomputation -- mirrors OBJ loader's recompute-always approach but preserves valid STL normals for efficiency
- `u32::try_from(idx)` used for all index conversions (not bare `as u32`) to guard against overflow on 32-bit platforms
