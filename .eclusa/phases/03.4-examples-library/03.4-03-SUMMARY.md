- **Runtime path resolution over `include_str!`** — follows model-viewer's `std::fs::read_to_string(CARGO_MANIFEST_DIR + relative)` pattern; avoids `include_str!`'s directory-depth-counting pitfall (RESEARCH Pitfall 4).
- **Ship cube-only recipe (no mesh rendering)** — per RESEARCH F-05 / D-08, `walk_and_render` dispatches Cube/Layer/Group only; mesh recipe rendering is post-v1 backlog. The bundled `hello.json` is already cube-only.
- **Rejected-path block left commented-out** — per D-07, the example renders cleanly by default; uncommenting the block demonstrates the sandbox rejecting `../../etc/passwd` with a stderr log line.
- **Explicit `asset_root: PathBuf::from(ASSET_ROOT)`** rather than `SandboxConfig::default()` — makes the sandbox boundary visible at the call site for educational value.
